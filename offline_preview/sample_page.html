What this project does

A full-stack queue management system for a diagnostic centre. Patients are registered at reception, automatically routed through test rooms (Sonography, Blood Collection, X-Ray, etc.), and their status is shown on a TV display. Built with Next.js 15 (web) and Fastify + Prisma (API).
